English Level Test

Answer the following questions to the best of your ability.

1. Can you understand and use familiar everyday expressions and very basic phrases?

2. Can you communicate in simple and routine tasks requiring a direct exchange of information?

3. Can you understand the main points of clear standard input on familiar matters regularly encountered in work, school, or leisure?

4. Can you produce clear, detailed text on a wide range of subjects?

5. Can you understand a wide range of demanding, longer texts, and recognize implicit meaning?
